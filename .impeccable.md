## Design Context

### Users
Primary users are recruiters and hiring managers evaluating technical depth, product sense, and communication clarity. The interface should help them scan quickly while still feeling polished and memorable.

### Brand Personality
Premium, editorial, refined. The site should feel intentional, calm, and confident rather than flashy or experimental.

### Aesthetic Direction
Support both light and dark themes with default dark mode. Keep palette cohesion across sections so each mode feels like a single canvas. Contact area should use a compact, dense card treatment with clean alignment and reduced clutter.

### Design Principles
1. Maintain cross-section chromatic consistency in each theme.
2. Use contrast through typography and spacing, not isolated background color blocks.
3. Keep interaction states subtle and premium.
4. Favor compact information grouping in contact and utility surfaces.
5. Preserve readability and scannability for recruiters on desktop and mobile.
